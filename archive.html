AAVE Galaxy

Three.js NLP 2024

3D galaxy where each film is a constellation of orbiting word-spheres. Sphere size encodes term frequency; white spheres are terms shared across 3+ films. Cluster distance reflects cosine similarity — semantically closer films sit nearer each other. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, click a cluster to focus.

AAVE Lexical & Similarity Tables

Data Table NLP 2024

Four-tab interactive table surfacing per-film AAVE metrics: density overview, detailed term breakdowns by film, top-5 most frequent terms, and cosine similarity scores against a tweet corpus. Low similarity scores across the board suggest that embedding models collapse dialectal variation — AAVE text lands in roughly the same semantic neighborhood as standard English regardless of lexical density.
